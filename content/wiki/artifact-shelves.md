Artifact Shelves consisted of retro and pop-culture d√©cor, framing Chuck E. as part of pop-culture history and fame. These shelves were placed on both sides of the stage and had various themes, with sometimes each side having a different one.**

- Hollywood
- Broadway
- Sports
- Music
- Wild West
